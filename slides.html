Welcome to Powerslide!

Navigate the Presentation with your mobile device!

A new way to control your presentation!

  • Present from anywhere with internet access. No software required!
  • Use your smart phone, keyboard or mouse to navigate the presentation
  • Create your own presentation with html

How does it work?

Create slides Using HTML

With a little HTML knowledge, create slides inside of sections named "class" 

<section class="slide">
	<h1>My Awesome Slide</h1>
</section>

How does it work?

Animate your slide with CSS3

simply adding a "slide" class to your section or div tag adds a fancy animation to your slides.

<section class="slide">
	<h1>My Awesome Slide</h1>
</section>

What else can you do?

Show Interesting Quotes

“Talk is cheap. Show me the code.”
― Linus Torvalds

Its as simple as putting a blockquote tag

<blockquote>“Talk is cheap. Show me the code.”― <a href="#">Linus Torvalds</a></blockquote>
